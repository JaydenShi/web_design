ABOUT US

Hi! We’re Adam and hailey, an American couple on a journey to travel the world while working from the road. We’re passionate about outdoor adventure, responsible travel, and getting off the beaten path. Along the journey, we have so many creative thinking about starting our own business. Eventually, our dreams come true, we established our first Airbnb staycation company called Sands Lodge.We welcome new friends come to our properties to experience the unique moment with your love ones.

OUR STORY

Sands Lodge started in the summer of 2021.The name of Sands lodge is inspired by spectacular desert view in Canyonlands National Park where our rental properties locate in. We are trying to deliver unique adventures for those people who want to leave hustle and bustle of the city. In general, there are two types of unique "room" available now. One of them are dome house while another is the motorhomes.People stay in domes or motorhomes will enjoy the spectacular mountain views.

OUR SERVICES

We do provide all the necessary amenities in our properties ,stunning desert views, as well as free cancellation due to covid-19 pandemic.
