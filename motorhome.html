About The Motorhome

Our brand new Motorhome is locate at Canyonlands,Utah.It is just 3 minutes walk to our dome house! It provides you the perfect opportunity for scenic hikes and exploring the national park. Motorhome also comes with desert views,stunning sunrises and sunsets.You can enjoy your breakfast on our wooden deck with stunning views,to grill your food at built-in BBQ area with propane grill. Our convenient outside facilities,including two large wooden decks,outdoor shower, dining table with chair ,abundant shade,and your very own fireplace,which all surrounded by the beautiful natural landscape. Our Motorhome is equipped with a queen size bed and couch that can be converted into a full size bed.
