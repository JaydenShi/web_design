About the dome house

Do you want to escape from hustle and bustle of the city? Come to our dome house to enjoy the cozy and quiet staycation. Enjoy the ourdoors without sacrificing the comfort of a king size bed, private bathroom , free WIFI and Barbecue utensils while staying in our cosy dome house.All the bathroom essentials will be provided during your staycation. What's more,pets are allowed in our domes as well. There is a Barbecue pit just right outside of the dome house. You will be able to park in the parking lot near the dome house, and walk in to your path. You will have digital key code to access to your dome when you arrive. Feel free to explore the property with its stunning mountain peaks.You may even spot white dogs and some cute goats.
